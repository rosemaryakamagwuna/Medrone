SAFE, RELIABLE, FAST AND PRECISE

As a delivery company operating in all states in Nigeria, Medrone brings a unique combination of industry-leading technology and expertise to meet your delivery needs.

At Medrone we deliver quick medical services using sophisticated drones and therefore, put people before profit. Our services include providing medical services that is delivered at your doorsteps and providing adequate medical care by our experts to ensure good health and well-being.

MEDICAL EMERGENCY DRONE

At Medrones, we know emergencies are where minutes count, we operate from distribution centers placed at the center of each region of service. Part medical warehouse, part drone airport, each distribution center can make hundreds of deliveries each day to any point.

MEDICAL DELIVERIES

Medrones adds agility and responsiveness to your medical supplies. Deliver blood and other medical supplies on demand directly to the point of injury and within the time when it is most needed and that is when medical intervention is most critical. For humanitarian assistance or disaster response missions, Medrone offers the capability to rapidly make hundreds of deliveries throughout the area of need, despite roads and pathways that may be damaged or impassable.
